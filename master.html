Safe Words

Safe words are essential for maintaining consent and communication during BDSM activities. Commonly used safe words include:

Green: Everything is okay, continue.

Yellow: Slow down or check-in; something is uncomfortable.

Red: Stop immediately; the activity must end.

Make sure both partners clearly agree on the safe words before starting any scene.

Aftercare

Aftercare is the time taken after a scene to ensure both partners feel emotionally and physically supported. It can include:

  • Providing water, snacks, or a blanket.
  • Gently cuddling or holding each other.
  • Discussing the scene and sharing feelings.
  • Taking care of any physical marks or injuries.

Aftercare is unique to every individual, so discuss and plan it beforehand with your partner.
